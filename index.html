Our Process

  • Consult & Plan
  • Concept & Design
  • Execute & Coordinate
  • Install & Finalize
Meeting: Discuss the client's needs, preferences, budget, and timeline. Site Assessment: Visit the space to understand its layout and limitations. Agreement: Establish a formal contract outlining the project's scope, fees, and timeline.
Conceptualization: Develop initial design concepts, including mood boards, sketches, and ideas. Client Feedback: Present the concepts to the client, refine them based on feedback. Design Development: Finalize detailed designs, including floor plans, material selection, and 3D renderings if needed.
Project Management: Coordinate with contractors, suppliers, and other professionals to bring the design to life. Supervision: Oversee the construction and installation process, ensuring everything aligns with the design. Adjustments: Make any necessary tweaks during the execution phase to address unforeseen challenges.
Installation: Complete the installation of furniture, fixtures, and accessories according to the design plan. Final Touches: Style the space, add finishing details, and ensure everything is perfect. Client Review: Conduct a final walkthrough with the client to ensure satisfaction, and address any last-minute concerns.

About-Us

Sagar Sarvankar

INTERIOR DESIGNER | PLANNER | CONSULTANT

At Bee Hive Design Studio, we believe that interior design is a reflection of how you live and what you love. Our approach focuses on capturing the essence of our clients through thoughtful use of light, color, shape, and texture, setting the perfect stage for their lives to unfold seamlessly. We prioritize direct communication, transparency, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs.

Founded by Sagar Sarvankar, a passionate interior designer with a strong background in the architectural product industry, Bee Hive Design Studio combines technical expertise with creative vision. Sagar's early career in client management, production, and installation, along with his Interior Designing Diploma from L.S. Raheja College in Mumbai, laid the foundation for his success.

Before establishing Bee Hive Design Studio, Sagar contributed to numerous prestigious projects, including residential and commercial spaces for renowned clients such as D B Realty, The Leela, The Lalit, ITC Grand Maratha, and high-profile individuals like Farhan Akhtar and Namrata Shirodkar. His diverse experience in windows, glass facades, architectural hardware, and railings allows him to seamlessly blend design and execution.
